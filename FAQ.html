FAQ - Shoe Store

1. How do I choose the right shoe size?

Choosing the right shoe size is important for comfort. We recommend measuring your feet and using our size chart to find the perfect fit.

2. What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unused and undamaged shoes. Please check our return policy page for more details.

3. How long does shipping take?

Shipping times may vary, but most orders are delivered within 5-7 business days. You can track your order using the provided tracking number.
